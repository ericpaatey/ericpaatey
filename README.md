## About

Throughout my career, I have led the development of mission-critical web applications, and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems for large organizations, including the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) of the United Nations (RAF Office, Ghana); Volta River Authority (Ghana's largest power utility corporation); Institute of Internal Auditors Ghana; Ghana Statistical Services; Ghana College of Nurses and Midwives; as well as other key businesses and non-profit institutions.

These experiences equip me with strong problem-solving abilities, a deep understanding of system reliability, and the discipline to build solutions that support both users and business goals.
